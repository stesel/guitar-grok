# Horror Metal Solo Writing in Drop B

## Introduction

This lesson focuses on writing horror-inspired metal solos in **Drop B tuning**:

```text
B F# B E G# C#
(low → high)
```

The goal is not maximum speed.

The goal is to create:

- Tension
- Fear
- Unease
- Madness
- Suspense
- Release

Think of your solo as telling a horror story rather than showing off technique.

---

# 1. The Three Most Important Scales

## B Natural Minor

B C# D E F# G A B

## B Harmonic Minor

B C# D E F# G A# B

Important movement:

A# → B

## B Phrygian

B C D E F# G A B

Important movement:

B → C → B

---

# 2. The Two Essential Horror Intervals

## Minor Second

B - C

Example:

```text
B|--0-1-0--
```

## Tritone

B - F

Example:

```text
C#|--5-----
B |--0-----
```

# 7. Horror Metal Solo Formula

70% B Natural Minor / B Harmonic Minor

20% B → C → B

10% Tritones and chromatic notes

# Final Rule


